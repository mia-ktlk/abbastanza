About Us

Abbastanza was founded by Jennifer Kotalik with the mission of changing the conversation around body image and acceptance. Starting small, her team is growing into a dedicated community who work together to encourage wellness from within. We work to combat eating disorders, body dysmorphia, and other mental health problems caused by the myth of body perfection. Through education, counseling, and community outreach, we at Abbastanza strive to help members of our community know that they are enough.

UPCOMMING EVENTS

NEDA Walk

April 18, 2020 | Phoenix, Arizona

Since 2009, NEDA Walks have united communities, while raising awareness and funds for NEDA’s life-saving programs! The National Eating Disorders Association (NEDA) is the leading nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting individuals and families affected by eating disorders, and serving as a catalyst for prevention, cures, and access to quality care. By signing up you will be supporting me as well as the thousands of people who rely on NEDA’s programs and resources every day. Together we can make a difference in the fight against eating disorders!
