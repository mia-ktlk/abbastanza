About US

Abbastanza was founded by Jennifer Kotalik with the mission of changing the conversation around body image and acceptance. Starting small, her team is growing into a dedicated community who work together to encourage wellness from within. We work to combat eating disorders, body dysmorphia, and other mental health problems caused by the myth of body perfection. Through education, counseling, and community outreach, we at Abbastanza strive to make members of our community know that they are enough.
